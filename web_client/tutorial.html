Get Started !

scholarScape is designed to make your life easy when dealing with Google Scholar. It enables you to launch crawling campaigns on a particular word, a particular author or a particular URL (you can put several separated by break lines). scholarScape will then :

  • store all the publications on this page
  • click on all the cited by "links" and fetch all the publications on those pages too
  • and again until a certain depth has been reached

Organisation

The are two main pages in scholarScape : launch and explore.

The launch section is where you launch your crawling campaigns. You only have to fill in the project your campaign will belong to, your campaign name, the start titles/authors/urls, the depth of the crawl and the download_delay.
